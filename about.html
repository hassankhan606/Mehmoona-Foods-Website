16.10.2020:The Begin of Mehmoona Foods.

Founded with a passion for authentic flavors and traditional recipes, Mehmoona Foods began as a small family venture specializing in pure spices and herbal remedies. What started as a humble home-based business grinding fresh spices has grown into a trusted brand across Pakistan. From our first batches of Red Chilli Powder and Turmeric to our comprehensive range of herbal products, every step has been guided by quality and tradition.

Our journey began with the vision to bring pure, unadulterated spices to Pakistani kitchens. The secret family recipes for our Garam Masala and Chaat Masala became the foundation of our brand. We carefully sourced the finest chillies, coriander, and other ingredients to create blends that would enhance every meal while staying true to traditional flavors.

16.10.2021:Expanding Our Herbal Range.

Recognizing the growing demand for natural remedies, we introduced our herbal line starting with pure Ishphagol (Psyllium Husk) and our special Digestive Tea blend. These products quickly gained popularity for their purity and effectiveness. We maintained strict quality control, ensuring our herbal products were free from additives and preservatives, just as nature intended.

At Mehmoona Foods, our spices are carefully selected, cleaned, and ground to preserve their natural aroma and potency. Whether it's our vibrant Red Chilli Powder, golden Turmeric, or aromatic Dhaniya Powder, each product undergoes rigorous quality checks. Our special blends like Garam Masala and Chaat Masala are crafted using traditional recipes that have been perfected over generations.

16.10.2022:Innovation in Tradition.

We introduced our premium Special Tea blend and expanded our herbal range with Neem Powder, a powerful Ayurvedic herb. Our commitment to innovation while preserving traditional knowledge helped us create unique products that catered to modern health-conscious consumers without compromising on authenticity.

Our mission at Mehmoona Foods is to bring health and flavor to every Pakistani home. We believe that good food begins with quality ingredients. That's why we're committed to providing pure, authentic spices and herbal products that help families cook nutritious, flavorful meals while also offering natural wellness solutions.

16.10.2023:National Recognition.

This year marked our expansion to national distribution, with our products now available across major cities in Pakistan. We received recognition for our commitment to quality and traditional methods, particularly for our special spice blends and herbal remedies that maintained authentic flavors and benefits.

We continued to innovate by introducing convenient packaging options while maintaining our strict quality standards. Our focus remained on providing the freshest spices and most effective herbal products, from our signature Red Chilli Powder to our therapeutic Ishphagol and special tea blends.

16.10.2024:Sustainability Initiatives.

We implemented sustainable sourcing practices, working directly with local farmers to procure the best chillies, turmeric, and coriander. Our herbal products like Neem Powder and Ishphagol were now being used not just in homes but also recommended by herbal practitioners across the country.

Mehmoona Foods became synonymous with trust in the spice and herbal market. Our Dhaniya Powder and Garam Masala became household staples, while our special tea blend developed a loyal following. We remained committed to our core values - purity, tradition, and customer satisfaction.

16.10.2025:Looking Ahead.

Today, Mehmoona Foods stands as a proud Pakistani brand offering a complete range of spices and herbal products. From our essential spice powders to our therapeutic herbal offerings, each product carries our promise of quality. As we move forward, we continue to innovate while staying rooted in the traditions that made us successful.

With dedication to quality and love for authentic flavors at its core, Mehmoona Foods has transformed from a small local business into a trusted national brand. From hand-pounding small batches to modern production while maintaining traditional methods, our focus remains unchanged - delivering the true taste and benefits of Pakistan's rich culinary and herbal heritage.
